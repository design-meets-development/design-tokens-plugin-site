    FAQs

    Can I include the notes in my exported document?

    Sure! The annotations are made with native Sketch layers. Just make sure that the notes are visible when you are exporting and they will appear in the final doc.

    Can I get a trial version of the plugin?

    No, but if the plugin doesn't fit your needs drop us a line and we'll discuss a refund for your purchase.

    Can I get the plugin for free if I'm a student?

    Sure. Send us an email using your student account and you'll get a version with basic features.

    How can I upgrade from my previous Notebook Plugin version?

    If you downloaded the plugin recently, contact us and we'll send you a redemption code. Remember that free upgrades are only available to premium users.

    Can I buy licenses in bulk at different princing?

    Yes, contact us and we will give you the details regarding this matter.

    Is it possible to develop a custom version of this plugin?

    Yes. Contact us and lets discuss what kind of plugin we can develop for you: company styles, advanced features, workflow integrations... You can also make inquiries about new plugin developments.

    What can I do if the plugin stops working after an Sketch update?

    Don't panic. We usually release an update around the same date as Sketch does. You should receive an email with a download link shortly after that happens. If not, please make sure that you don't have any filter blocking our emails and that they're not sent directly to the spam folder or trash bin.

    Why I didn't receive any link after my purchase?

    Make sure that you are checking the email account that you entered when downloading the plugin and that there isn't any filter or proxy blocking our communications. If you don't get it please email us and we'll solve the issue for you.
